Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 21, 2026

TempNote does not collect, transmit, or store any of your data on a server. Everything you create is either removed once you close the website, via Scratchpad or stored locally in your browser using localStorage, via Stash.

What this means:

  • No account is required to use this app.
  • We do not collect your notes, as they never leave your device.
  • We do not use cookies, analytics, or trackings of any kind.
  • Clearing your browser's cache will permanently delete your notes. Since we never had a copy, we cannot recover them.
  • Using a different browser or device will not show the same notes.

Third Parties:

This app does not integrate with any third-party services, ad networks, or analytics providers. No data is shared with anyone.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. If app's functionality changes in a way that can affect your privacy, this policy will be updated accordingly. You are advised to review this privacy policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
